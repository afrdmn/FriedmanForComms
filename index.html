About Me!

My name is Aaron Friedman, and I’m running to become your next Communications Director for HSDA. Over the past year, I’ve served as the Communications Director for the New Jersey High School Democrats.

National

I’m the Executive Editor for the Progressive Teen’s Podcast: TPT Radio. This role has entailed distributing, editing, mixing, and promoting the podcast. I've worked with many different stakeholders to get the podcast out in an extremely timely manner.

State

In one short year, I have entirely revamped NJHSD’s outreach and multiplied every digital engagement metric in every way possible.

Local

At the end of sophomore year, I became increasingly active in local politics, becoming a fellow for two local State Assembly campaigns. At the same time, I co-founded and led the HSDA chapter for my school (one of the largest chapters in New Jersey). I was instrumental in coordinating robust canvassing efforts with 70+ high school students for those races during GOTV weekend by assembling a massive database of names and phone numbers and sending out nightly blasts with information. Because the majority of these students don’t yet have their driver’s license, I helped coordinate fleets of transportation in the form of my County Democratic Party’s vans, as well as student drivers, to make these colossal efforts possible and get kids involved.
